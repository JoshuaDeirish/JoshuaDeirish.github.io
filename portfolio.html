About My Projects

My software development journey began in 2015. At the time, my projects lacked the polish and sophistication that would capture anyone's attention. However, I was determined to continually improve my skills and vowed to surpass my previous projects with each new one. Whenever I made a mistake, I diligently took note of it and used the experience to better myself on the next project. As I gained more confidence, I began sharing my work on Github.

Since then, I have taken on medium and large-scale projects, both as an independent developer and as part of a team. Regardless of the project, I always commit myself fully and motivate those around me to do the same.
