About me

Greetings! My name is Joshua De Irish and I hail from Durham Region in Ontario, Canada. Ever since I was a child, I have been passionate about exploring my creativity and experimenting with various forms of art. While I initially aspired to become a full-time artist, I eventually realized that I needed to channel my creativity into a more lucrative career path. As I began searching for opportunities to do so, I stumbled upon the world of software development. This field has not only allowed me to utilize my creative skills but has also provided me with a fulfilling and financially rewarding career.

Software development has allowed me to explore my creativity in exciting new ways. Programming has also taught me valuable life lessons, including the importance of being open-minded when it comes to problem-solving. One of my favorite lessons is that there is no single solution to any given problem. This mindset has proved useful both in my personal and professional life. Moving forward, I aspire to use my skills and knowledge to help others while also enhancing my work. I am eager to continue applying the lessons I have learned and to remain open to new opportunities for growth and development.
